Instant Pot Hamburger Soup

Easy and delicious soup

You can make it in the Instant PotÂ® or on the stovetop. When this cools, it will be thick, but it thins out when reheated. Freezes very well.

Easy to make and little ingredients required, and is still delicious!

Ingredients Required

  • 1 1/2 pounds ground beef
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cans beef consomme
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 can condensed tomato soup
  • 4 carrots, finely chopped
  • 3 stalks celery, finely chopped
  • 4 tablespoons pearl barley
  • 1/2 teaspoons dried thyme
  • 1 bay leaf

Cooking Instructions

  1. Use a multi-funcional cooker. Cook and stir beef and onion until browned, 5 to 10 minutes. Pour in beef consomme, tomatoes, water, and tomato soup. Add carrots, celery, barley, thyme, and bay leaf.
  2. Close and lock the lid. Select Soup function; set timer for 30 minutes. Allow 10 to 15 minutes for pressure to build.
  3. Release pressure using the natural-release method according to manufacturer's instructions, about 10 minutes.
